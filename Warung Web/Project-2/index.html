Welcome to Made With Love!

Welcome to "Made With Love", a charming farm-to-table restaurant located in the heart of the countryside.

As you step inside, you are greeted by a warm and rustic ambiance, with exposed wooden beams and natural stone walls that will transport you to a simpler time.

Our Menus

Asian Glazed Grilled Salmon

Asian Glazed Grilled Salmon is a dish that features a juicy and flaky salmon fillet, grilled to perfection and coated with a sweet and savory Asian-inspired glaze. The glaze is made with a combination of soy sauce, honey, garlic, ginger, and sesame oil, which gives the salmon a delicious umami flavor with a hint of sweetness.

Chocolate Eruption Cake

Indulge in the rich, decadent taste of our Chocolate Eruption Cake! This moist and fluffy chocolate cake is filled with a molten chocolate center that oozes out with every bite, creating an explosion of flavor in your mouth. The lava-like chocolate is made from high-quality dark chocolate, providing a rich and intense taste that is sure to satisfy any chocolate lover's cravings.

Rich Chocolate Fantasy

Rich Chocolate Fantasy is a luxurious chocolate ice cream that will satisfy any chocolate lover's cravings. Made with the finest cocoa powder and rich cream, this ice cream is ultra-smooth and creamy. The chocolate flavor is intense and indulgent, with a slight bitterness that balances the sweetness perfectly.
